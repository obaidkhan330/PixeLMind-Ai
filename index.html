✦ FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is PixelMind AI completely free? +
Yes! PixelMind AI is 100% free. You use your own Google Gemini API key which has a generous free quota. No payment to us ever. Google's free tier allows up to 15 requests/minute.
How do I get a Gemini API key? +
Go to Google AI Studio (aistudio.google.com), sign in with your Google account, click "Get API Key", create a new key, and copy it. Paste it in the Generate page. It's completely free!
Is my API key safe? +
Yes! Your API key is stored only in your browser's localStorage. It never leaves your device or gets sent to our servers. PixelMind calls the Gemini API directly from your browser.
How many images can I generate? +
The number depends on your Gemini API quota. Google's free tier allows up to 15 images per minute and 1500 per day. Premium Google accounts have higher limits. PixelMind itself imposes no limits.
What image styles are available? +
We offer 8 styles: Realistic/Photorealistic, Anime/Manga, 3D Render, Digital Art, Oil Painting, Watercolor, Pencil Sketch, and Cyberpunk. All are free!
Can I use generated images commercially? +
Images are generated by Google Gemini API. Please review Google's Gemini API Terms of Service for commercial usage rights. Generally, images you generate can be used for most purposes.
